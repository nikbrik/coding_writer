# Defer Log

Task: record the concrete task name.
Task ID: record the stable scope-lock task id.

## Deferred Items

### `DEFER-001`

Status: `open | kept | changed | reverted | deferred-further`

Location/context:

- File, module, behavior, or decision point.

Unresolved uncertainty:

- What was not fully known during execution.

Conservative choice taken:

- Smallest safe choice used during execution.

Alternative:

- Reasonable alternative the user may prefer.

Rollback/change path:

- Concrete edits or decisions needed to switch to the alternative.

Resolution:

- User outcome and follow-up action.
